White Belt

▼

"First Steps on the Mat"

🎯 Target

0-1,000 subscribers
10+ videos published

📈 Focus

Foundation building
Consistency & basics

📚 Skills to Master

  • Establish consistent upload schedule (weekly/bi-weekly)
  • Master basic video editing and ensure good audio quality
  • Define your niche and understand your target audience
  • Create compelling thumbnails and titles
  • Learn YouTube Studio basics and analytics
🎬 First Upload 📅 30-Day Streak 🎯 Niche Defined

🛠️ Essential Resources

📱 Mobile Editing
Start with free apps like CapCut, InShot, or iMovie for basic editing on your phone.
🎨 Thumbnail Tools
Canva, Figma, or Photoshop for creating eye-catching thumbnails that get clicks.
🎤 Audio Setup
Invest in a basic USB microphone like Blue Yeti or Audio-Technica ATR2100x.
📊 YouTube Studio
Master the Creator Studio dashboard, analytics, and community tab features.

🗺️ 90-Day Roadmap

Month 1: Focus on consistency. Upload weekly, even if quality isn't perfect. Learn your equipment.

Month 2: Improve production quality. Study successful channels in your niche. Engage with your audience.

Month 3: Analyze your best-performing videos. Double down on what works. Prepare for Blue Belt skills.

🏆 Belt Test: Successfully maintain uploads for 3 months straight with measurable improvement in quality and engagement.

Blue Belt

▼

"Building Your Foundation"

🎯 Target

1,000-10,000 subscribers
Monetization enabled

📈 Focus

Community growth
Content optimization

📚 Skills to Master

  • Master community engagement and respond to all comments
  • Learn basic SEO and keyword research for video optimization
  • Significantly improve production value (lighting, framing, editing)
  • Create your first viral video (relative to your channel size)
  • Build email list or presence on secondary platforms
💰 Monetized 🔥 First Viral 👥 1K Community

🛠️ Growth Resources

🔍 SEO Tools
TubeBuddy, VidIQ, or Keyword Tool for optimizing titles and tags.
💡 Lighting
Softbox lights or ring lights for professional-looking videos.
📧 Email Marketing
Mailchimp, ConvertKit, or Beehiiv for building your email audience.
📱 Social Media
Cross-promote on Instagram, TikTok, Twitter to build multi-platform presence.

🗺️ Growth Strategy

Optimization Phase: Use analytics to identify your best content. Create more of what works.

Community Building: Respond to every comment. Create content that encourages engagement.

Expansion: Start building presence on other platforms. Begin email list collection.

🏆 Belt Test: Achieve 1,000 true fans who regularly engage with your content and enable monetization.

Each belt has 4 stripes to mark progression between ranks:

🥈 2nd Stripe: Master 2/5 key skills

🥉 3rd Stripe: Master 4/5 key skills

🏆 4th Stripe: Complete the belt test + mentor someone at a lower belt
