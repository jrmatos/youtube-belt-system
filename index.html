Master the platform one belt at a time

White Belt

"First Steps on the Mat"

Milestone:

0-1,000 subscribers
10+ videos published

Skills Mastered:
  • Consistent upload schedule (weekly/bi-weekly)
  • Basic video editing and audio quality
  • Understanding your niche and target audience
  • Creating thumbnails and titles
  • Learning YouTube Studio basics
Belt Test:

Successfully maintain uploads for 3 months straight

Blue Belt

"Building Your Foundation"

Milestone:

1,000-10,000 subscribers
Monetization enabled

Skills Mastered:
  • Community engagement (responding to comments consistently)
  • Basic SEO and keyword research
  • Improved production value (lighting, framing, editing flow)
  • First viral video (relative to your size)
  • Building email list or secondary platform presence
Belt Test:

Achieve 1,000 true fans who regularly engage with your content

Purple Belt

"Finding Your Flow"

Milestone:

10,000-100,000 subscribers
Sustainable income

Skills Mastered:
  • Advanced analytics interpretation and strategy pivots
  • Collaboration with other creators
  • Diversified content formats (shorts, long-form, live streams)
  • Brand partnerships and sponsorship deals
  • Community building (Discord, membership, etc.)
Belt Test:

Create a content series that consistently performs well over 6+ episodes

Brown Belt

"Mastering Your Craft"

Milestone:

100,000-1,000,000 subscribers
Multiple revenue streams

Skills Mastered:
  • Thought leadership in your niche
  • Team building (editors, thumbnail designers, assistants)
  • Advanced business strategy (products, courses, consulting)
  • Cross-platform dominance (TikTok, Instagram, podcast, etc.)
  • Mentoring other creators
Belt Test:

Launch a successful product/service that serves your audience beyond YouTube

Black Belt

"Master of the Platform"

Milestone:

1,000,000+ subscribers
Significant cultural impact

Skills Mastered:
  • Industry influence and trend-setting
  • Building lasting business empire around your brand
  • Creating systems that work without your constant input
  • Teaching and elevating other creators
  • Adapting to platform changes and staying relevant long-term
Black Belt Achievement:

Your content has meaningfully impacted thousands of lives and created lasting value beyond entertainment
